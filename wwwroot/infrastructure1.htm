Infrastructure

We have our in-house dyeing plant for colouring and processing fabrics. We do outsource for additional ornamental works like printing, embroidery, etc., through our reliable business channels.

Dyeing & treatment plant

Our dyeing unit comprises of most modern environmental friendly dyeing machines which is one of the modern dye house situated in Tirupur. We have also installed the latest Reverse Osmosis and Mutliple Evaporator plant to recycle the effluent water and reuse the same water for the dye process there by saving the environment to a large extent and therefore saving a lot of water.

Our dyeing unit is most renowned for most of the Universities and colleges in Tamilnadu State visiting our dyeing factory as a center for in-plant training and study about the technologies, procedures used in the fabric dyeing.

Environment Policy

We strive to protect, conserve and restore the natural environment for the benefit of future generations by:

  • Conducting its operations in an environmentally responsible manner.
  • Preventing pollution in compliance with relevant environmental legislation and regulations.
  • Ensuring continual improvement in its environmental performance by setting targets.
  • Enhancing environmental awareness amongst its employees and suppliers.
Garment Manufacturing

We have installed the latest Pegasus, Siruba and Juki sewing from Japan to sew the garments to perfection.

Planned work flow and quality control systems ensure better finish. Each garment is physically checked before dispatch. Our cutting and pattern masters, supervisors, tailors and checking team are well trained in professional work culture.

We have extensive arrangements for hand embroidery, sequin work, beads work etc. Our value addition includes exciting hand work to make the garment look trendy and current.

Easy accessibility to fabric & accessories from all over India.

Quality Process

We take 100% efforts to ensure our products are of defect free.
Here are some compelling reasons for you to buy from us.

  • Consistent high quality throughout the production volume
  • Timely commitment to deliver goods at the right price
  • We ensure using of quality raw materials
  • Usage of state-of-the-art technology
  • Three layer inspection of each production

We check the following parameters at each stage ;

Fibre :
  • Moisture Content
  • Staple Length
  • Maturity of the Fibre
 Yarn :
  • Count Strength Product
  • Black Board test
  • Twist Multiplier
  • Type of twist
 Fabric :
  • Color fastness in wet and dry condition.
  • Color fastness to perispiration and saliva
  • Dimensional stability.
  • Fabric Strength test
  • Spirality Test

We have got an efficient quality assurance team which looks into the intricacies of quality management through continuous online checks right from receipt of raw material to end product, using the latest quality monitoring machines, and ensuring that the end product meets buyer requirement.

Our goods are checked according to AQL standards.
